How we make a difference

We are a international, non-profit, Cross-Cultural Organization that advocates for Cultural-citizen Diplomacy, Peace building, youth empowerment and community progress. Our activitites Promote cross-cultural peace, bilateral relations and Inter-Nation purposeful engagments. FAO creates opportunities on a global scale for cross-cultural peace, developing young potentials, foreign cultural businesses networking and connecting people from around the world for global impact.

This is what we do

Health Awareness programs

We have spear-headed countless health awareness programs, targeted at those living in the rural parts of the country. This has done alot of good for those who would other wise have no means gaining access to information and treatment..

Relief Programs

We have partnered with the German goverment, in the provison and distribution of relief items to those less priveledged. Also we have organised food drives in the rural areas of the country, especially during economic down time.

Youth Empowerment

Youth empowerment and the ills of illegal migration are a core part of what our organisation is involved in. We have over the years organised programs, rehabilitations and seminars to assist the youths in the nation with help from international bodies.
